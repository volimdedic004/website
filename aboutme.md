Hello! I'm Daris Dedic, a first-year Mechanical Engineering student at George Washington University with a strong passion for Aerospace Engineering and making music.

I am currently a member of the aerostructures division for the GW Rocket team, where I occassionally help construct and manufacture components for our entry in the Spaceport America Cup. My technical toolkit includes hands-on 3D moedling and drafting using SolidWorks, OnShape, and AutoCAD, along with an established foundation in data science and computational analysis using Python, NumPy, and Pandas.

Whether I am just trying to get by troubleshooting a complex mechanical assembly, apply analysis to datasets, or make my next song, I enjoy using these skills to solve complex problems and establish strong connections wtih my peers. I pride myself on an interdisciplinary approach to my work and I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to apply my engineering/artistry skills to real-world, innovative projects.
